# Code of Conduct

Algoryth is an open-source project. We want a welcoming, respectful community.

## Our standards
- Be respectful and constructive
- Assume good intent
- Keep feedback focused on the work, not the person
- No harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks

## Scope
This applies to issues, pull requests, discussions, and other project spaces.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this code.
Repeated or severe behavior may result in a ban from the project spaces.
